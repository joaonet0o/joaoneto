Ted Talks Headquarters




TED Talks is headquartered in New York, a one of the most influential cities in the world, known for its cultural diversity and innovation. Since its founding in 1984, TED began as an annual conference that brought together technology leaders, entertainment and design. As the years passed, the organization has expanded significantly, transforming into a platform global platform for the dissemination of ideas and knowledge.



TED headquarters is the administrative heart of the organization, where a dedicated team works to plan and execute events, manage partnerships and develop content. The team is made up of professionals from different areas, including communication, marketing, event production and technology. This diversity allows TED to maintain its relevance and innovation, adapting to new trends and needs of the public.
